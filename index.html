Our Practice Area

Divorce

We help clients navigate Texas divorce laws, including property division, alimony, and contested or uncontested divorce proceedings.

Child Custody

We assist in establishing fair and sustainable parenting plans that maintain strong parent-child relationships while protecting parental rights.

Child Support

Our attorneys help clients establish, modify, and enforce child support orders to ensure children receive the financial care they need.

Post-Nuptial Agreements

We draft legally sound prenuptial and postnuptial agreements to safeguard assets, define financial expectations, and prevent future disputes.

Collaborative Divorce

We facilitate structured negotiations, helping clients reach fair agreements without courtroom litigation.

Mediation
